Carey's Leadership Framework

The way we lead matters because it impacts people and all people matter.

Our framework is shaped by our core convictions of what we believe our leaders do: they deepen our faith; help shape our vision; form our culture; foster community; and ensure excellent practice.

How leaders go about their task is equally important. At Carey our leaders display courage in the face of complexity and challenge; value creativity; are accountable; are aware of the need for sustainable practices; and operate in a way which is respectful, compassionate and kind.

The intersection of these provides a rich vocabulary to characterise leadership at Carey. These words and concepts help us evaluate if we are on target as we aspire to provide the leadership Carey desires and needs.

This framework words together with our Theological Architecture and Carey Voice documents, and the values and convictions expressed there should work together with the Leadership Framework when making decisions. All of these are enriched in an environment that values seeing and deep listening.
